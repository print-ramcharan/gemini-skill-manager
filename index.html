About

Gemini Skill Manager

Version 1.0.0

A desktop utility to manage your AI assistant's active skill set and optimize context token usage.

Help

Getting Started

The Skill Manager lets you control which skills are active for your AI assistant. Active skills consume context tokens — keeping only what you need improves performance and prevents truncation.

Dashboard

Your main workspace. Skills are organized by category with master toggles. Click the chevron (›) on any category to expand it and manage individual skills.

Quick Presets

One-click presets configure your skills for common workflows: Full-stack Web, Mobile, AI/ML, DevOps, Security, and more. Enable "Limit to 20k" to keep presets within the token safety zone.

Token Budget

The gauge in the stats panel shows how much context space your active skills consume. The safety limit is 20,000 tokens — exceeding it may cause the assistant to truncate skill descriptions.

  • Green — Within limit, optimal performance
  • Orange — Approaching limit, consider deactivating skills
  • Red — Over limit, truncation likely
